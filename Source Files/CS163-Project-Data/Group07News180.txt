Hotels in Ho Chi Minh City


Ho Chi Minh City is the largest city in Vietnam and also also one with the most visitor count on a yearly basis. Tan Son Nhat airport is located about 7km from the city center of District 1 and most hotels in Ho Chi Minh City are allocated accordingly. District 1, especially Dong Khoi and Pham Ngu Lao area, sees the largest crowd with un-uniformity in both quality, price ranges and room services. One can find every hotel for his or her pocket, from the legendary Rex Hotel to the basic at the lower end. Near the airport, there are also many hotels built for your needs and the same can be spoken about other populous areas such as Cho Lon (China Town) and District 7.
